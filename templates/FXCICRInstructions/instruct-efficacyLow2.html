Instructions

The other way you can get coins is RANDOMLY, these coins are NOT based on your performance

Instead RANDOM coins will be based on random chance (like a lottery).

During the turn you will still see the tracker at the bottom of the board keeping track of the coins you would get based on your performance.

However at the end of the turn you will see the coins you have gotten RANDOMLY.

To help you remember that you are getting coins RANDOMLY, you will see a die before each turn.
