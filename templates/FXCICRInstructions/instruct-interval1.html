Instructions

The game will be broken up into turns, and each turn will be about 5 seconds long, so the faster you respond, the more words you can respond to.

Each turn will start with one of the colored words you saw earlier. Once you respond to that word, a new word will appear. This will keep happening until the time for that turn runs out.

You will no longer see the words ‘correct’ or ‘incorrect’ after each response.

Instead, at the bottom of the board you will see a tracker which will track the number of correct responses.

Importantly, you should continue to respond only to the INK COLOR of each word. You should NOT respond based on the content of the word.
